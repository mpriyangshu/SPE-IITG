üóìÔ∏è Held on 5th May 2025 | Organized by IIT Guwahati SPE Student Chapter

The IIT Guwahati SPE Student Chapter extends its sincere gratitude to all who contributed to the success of the one-day symposium on "The Role of Upstream Petroleum Engineering in Energy Transition," with a focused lens on Geothermal Energy and Geologic Carbon Sequestration (GCS).

This event, held on May 5, 2025, provided a dynamic platform to explore how traditional upstream disciplines such as reservoir characterization, fluid dynamics, and subsurface modeling are being redefined in light of the energy transition. Discussions delved into cutting-edge domains including Enhanced Geothermal Systems (EGS), machine learning applications in fracture network analysis, and integrated CCUS workflows.

Greetings from the IIT Guwahati SPE Student Chapter!

In collaboration with the Department of Chemical Engineering, IIT Guwahati, the IIT Guwahati SPE Student Chapter successfully organized the International Conference on Petroleum, Hydrogen, and Decarbonization (ICPHD 2023) from November 3 to 5, 2023.

The conference brought together distinguished plenary, keynote, and invited speakers, who shared their insights on critical developments in petroleum, hydrogen technologies, and decarbonization strategies. Their contributions enriched the dialogue around energy transition and inspired attendees to explore innovative, forward-thinking approaches.

Special thanks are extended to all participants and presenters whose research and discussions made ICPHD 2023 a truly impactful event.

We gratefully acknowledge the efforts of our convenors:
Prof. Pankaj Tiwari, Prof. Sumit Kumar, Ph.D., and Prof. Abhijit Kakati, along with the dedicated team of volunteers who made this event possible.

Empowering the Future of Drilling Automation

Through the collaboration of the SPE Drilling Systems Automation Technical Section (DSATS) and DrillDocs, an exhibition was successfully hosted during the International Conference on Petroleum, Hydrogen, and Decarbonization (ICPHD 2023), held from November 3 to 5, 2023, at IIT Guwahati.

As part of this initiative, DSATS-Asia conducted a quiz on drilling and automation aimed at raising awareness among students about the critical role of drilling systems automation in the petroleum industry. In addition, a presentation titled "Overview of SPE Drilling Systems Automation Technical Section (DSATS)" was delivered to introduce participants to the vision and mission of DSATS.

The DSATS-Asia team also engaged in meaningful discussions with professors, researchers, and industry professionals, highlighting the growing importance of automation and digital technologies in the energy sector.

We would like to extend our sincere gratitude to Mr. Dimitrios Pirovolou and Mr. Calvin Holt for their valuable guidance and mentorship, which played a vital role in the smooth execution of the exhibition.

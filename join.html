How to Join?

Step 1

It's FREE and open for ALL IITG students! Go to SPE International website (spe.org) and click 'Sign in'.

Step 2

If you don't have an account, click on "Register" to create a new account.

Step 3

Fill out the registration form with your details.

Step 4

Click 'Register' to create a new user account.

Step 5

Enter your personal email address and click 'Submit'.

Step 6

Fill out any field with a red asterisk to register. In address type you can select 'University Residence' and enter 'IIT Guwahati Amingaon' in the Address Line 1 and '781039' in the Postal Code. For your password, please set it to your student ID. Finally, click the 'Save' button.

Step 7

After redirecting to the login web page, fill your personal email address and password (student ID), then sign in.

Step 8

Complete your membership application.

Step 9

Select student membership option.

Step 10

Fill in your educational details and select IIT Guwahati as your institution.

Step 11

Review and confirm your membership details.

Step 12

Finally, Congratulations now you are an IITG SPE member!! Don't forget to contact the Membership Chairperson to request an exclusive group chat for members. If you have any problems, feel free to ask Mr. Nikhil J Amin (Membership Chairperson).

By becoming a member of IITG SPE, you are automatically enrolled as an SPE International member, gaining access to:

  1. Free or discounted SPE Online Education.
  2. SPE Connect – a global platform for professional collaboration and knowledge exchange.
  3. SPE Energy Stream content.
  4. Opportunities for international exposure through events such as SPE ATCE, IPTC, and other global conferences.
  5. Participation in international competitions like PetroBowl and Student Paper Contests.
  6. Extensive resources for networking, career development, and industry insights.
  7. Complimentary subscription to the Journal of Petroleum Technology (JPT).
  8. The Way Ahead (TWA) – a magazine for young professionals in the upstream sector.
  9. Access to selected technical papers through OnePetro.

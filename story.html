Our Vision

SPE IITG: Shaping the Future of Energy

The Department of Chemical Engineering at IIT Guwahati has established itself as a pioneer in energy, environment, and sustainability research, with a dedicated focus on advancing technologies for the petroleum sector, hydrogen economy, and decarbonisation.

Through strategic collaborations with industry leaders, government initiatives, and international academic institutions, our department is driving innovations that align with the global energy transition and India's ambitious mission towards net-zero emissions and clean energy solutions.

Our research expertise spans a comprehensive range of domains, from fundamental sciences including geosciences, reservoir engineering, and flow through porous media, to emerging frontiers such as carbon capture, hydrogen technologies, digitalisation, and unconventional resources. This breadth ensures our graduates and researchers are exceptionally equipped to address the complex challenges of today's energy landscape while developing sustainable solutions for future generations.

From Our Faculty Advisor

"The SPE Student Chapter at IIT Guwahati was founded in 2016 and began its formal operations in 2020. I distinctly recall establishing this chapter with the vision and support originating from both faculty members and students of the Department of Chemical Engineering at IIT Guwahati. Our shared commitment was to create a platform that bridges academic excellence with industry relevance in the petroleum engineering domain."

Dr. Sumit Kumar

Faculty Advisor, SPE IITG Student Chapter
Assistant Professor, Department of Chemical Engineering
